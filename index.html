Why does your dog need walks?

Daily dog walks are important for the health of your pup. Walking your dog provides them with mental stimulation, physical exercise,chances for socialisation and opportunities for behavioural training. A sedentary pooch can quickly become an overweight one and that brings potential health problems with it. You will benefit from having a well exercised dog as tired dogs tend to behave better. A walk helps stave off boredom which can lead to unwanted behaviours such as chewing or digging.

Why use a dog walker?

Whether you are too busy at work, or suffering any illness or injury, your dog needs to be walked so let us help you! Talk to us now, we can tailor a plan for you whether it's once off or regular daily walks!
